### Boss关
**短文**：I have a pet dog. Its name is Max. It is two years old. Max has brown fur and two big ears. It likes to run in the park and play with a ball. Every morning, I feed Max some dog food. In the evening, we go for a walk together. Max is my good friend.

1. What is the name of the pet? A. Tom  B. Max  C. Bob  D. Jack （答案：B）
2. What color is Max? A. black  B. white  C. brown  D. yellow （答案：C）
3. When do they go for a walk? A. morning  B. noon  C. evening  D. night （答案：C）
